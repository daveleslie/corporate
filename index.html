ABOUT US

Company

Ugu Engineering Solutions was founded in 2003 as a specialised retail energy management consultancy with the specific aim of addressing the need for technology driven energy efficiency solutions along with instituting best behavioral practices within large energy consumer organisations

Vision

We are firm believers in the need for better energy efficiency awareness and implementation in business and look to leverage the power of technology to align corporate environmental and social sustainability with financial bottom lines.

Mission

Our mission is to optimise energy consumption patterns and energy management practices within large businesses throughout Africa and the developed world. We aim to drive this by developing and implementing cutting edge hardware and software solutions, and providing turn-key energy management advisory services.
